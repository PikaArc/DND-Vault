## Current Events

Recent events in the Feywild have cast a shadow over Starfall Plaza. A mysterious celestial phenomenon, known as the Starfall, has captivated the city's residents, drawing crowds to the plaza in awe and wonder. Scholars and astrologers study the phenomenon, searching for clues to its meaning and significance.

## History

Starfall Plaza has been a focal point of Osfelin's cultural and spiritual life for generations. It is here that fey festivals are celebrated, royal decrees are proclaimed, and momentous occasions are marked with grand ceremonies. The plaza's history is steeped in legend and tradition, with tales of ancient rituals and mystical rites passed down through the ages.
